# Outreach Playbook

The article promised this and didn't deliver: **"如果你想知道'加上 WhatsApp 后，第一句话怎么发回复率最高'，请在评论区扣个【想看】"** — translation: "comment if you want it next time".

This file delivers it. First-message templates + 3-touch follow-up sequence + per-region tone notes.

---

## The first-message framework

Every cold WhatsApp message must do **all five** in 1–2 sentences:

1. **Identify yourself + company** (1 phrase)
2. **State where you found them** (defuses "who are you?")
3. **One-sentence value proposition tied to *their* business** (not yours)
4. **Single specific question** that needs a one-line answer
5. **Soft opt-out** ("if not relevant, no worries")

Bad (most cold messages):
> Hi, we are XYZ Lighting Co. We have been in LED industry 10 years and our products are top quality with best price. PSE FDA CE certified. Please find attached catalog and price list, looking forward to your reply. [PDF attachment]

Why it fails: zero context, brochure dump, no question, scream of "robot".

Good (this framework):
> Hi Rajesh — saw your contact on tradeindia.com under LED distributors in Mumbai. I work with [my company], we manufacture IP65 5050 strips at FOB Ningbo. Quick question: are you currently sourcing from China direct, or through a HK trading company? (No worries if not the right time.)

Why it works: named them, named source, specific product class, qualifying question, opt-out.

## First-message templates by buyer type

`{name}` `{source}` `{my_company}` `{product_specific}` `{your_company_or_role}` are merge fields.

### Buyer type: Importer / wholesale distributor (large volume)

**EN:**
> Hi {name} — found your contact on {source} listed as a {product_specific} distributor. I'm {sender_name} from {my_company}, we ship FOB {port} for {product_specific}. Quick question: are you currently sourcing direct from China or through a trading agent? Happy to share how our terms compare. (Reply STOP if you'd prefer no follow-up.)

**ES (LatAm):**
> Hola {name}, vi tu contacto en {source} como distribuidor de {product_specific}. Soy {sender_name} de {my_company}, exportamos {product_specific} desde China FOB Ningbo. Una pregunta rápida: ¿están comprando directo de fábrica en China o a través de un agente? Me gustaría compartir cómo se comparan nuestras condiciones. (Responde STOP si prefieres no recibir más mensajes.)

**PT (BR):**
> Olá {name}, encontrei seu contato em {source} como distribuidor de {product_specific}. Sou {sender_name} da {my_company}, fabricamos {product_specific} em Ningbo, China. Pergunta rápida: vocês compram direto da fábrica na China ou via trading? Posso compartilhar como nossos termos se comparam. (Responda PARAR caso prefira não receber mais mensagens.)

**DE:** ⚠️ Per `compliance.md` — do not run cold WhatsApp into Germany without legal review. Use email with double opt-in.

**FR:**
> Bonjour {name}, j'ai trouvé votre contact sur {source} comme distributeur de {product_specific}. Je suis {sender_name} de {my_company}, nous fabriquons des {product_specific} en Chine, livraison FOB Ningbo. Une question rapide : achetez-vous directement à l'usine en Chine ou via un intermédiaire ? Heureux de partager nos conditions. (Répondez STOP si vous préférez ne plus recevoir de messages.)

**AR (MENA):**
> مرحبا {name}، وجدت رقمك على {source} كموزع لـ {product_specific}. أنا {sender_name} من {my_company}، نصنع {product_specific} في الصين بشروط FOB نينغبو. سؤال سريع: هل تستوردون مباشرة من المصنع أم عبر وكيل تجاري؟ سعيد بمشاركة شروطنا. (للتوقف عن الرسائل، أرسل "إيقاف".)

### Buyer type: Retail / online seller (small volume, brand-conscious)

**EN:**
> Hi {name} — saw your shop on {source}. I'm {sender_name} from {my_company}, we make {product_specific} for small-batch Shopify and Etsy sellers (MOQ from 50). Curious: would private-label packaging be useful for you, or is generic fine? (No pressure — STOP to opt out.)

Tone: friendlier, smaller MOQ mentioned, brand angle hooked.

---

### Buyer type: Installer / contractor / end-user

**EN:**
> Hi {name} — found you via {source}, looks like you do {product_specific} installs. I'm {sender_name} from {my_company}, we supply contractors with {product_specific} at trade pricing (no minimum). Quick one: are you sourcing locally or open to direct China import for your next project? (Reply STOP to stop.)

Tone: contractor-friendly, "trade pricing", project-framed.

---

### Buyer type: Trade-show contact / catalog PDF lead

**EN:**
> Hi {name} — got your number from {source} (the {trade_show_or_doc_name} catalog). I'm {sender_name} from {my_company}, we manufacture {product_specific}. Quick check: is this number still the right one for sourcing inquiries, or should I direct to someone on your team? (STOP to opt out.)

Tone: lighter, "verify routing" — works because the lead is older.

## The 3-touch follow-up sequence

If no reply, follow up at **48h**, **5d**, **14d**. Then archive — do not chase past 14d, you're in spam territory.

### Touch 2 (48h after first message)

**EN:**
> Quick follow-up on my message — totally understand if WhatsApp isn't where you handle sourcing. Email or LinkedIn might be better. Just let me know which works (if any) and I won't keep messaging here.

Why it works: signals you're not a bulk-blast, gives them a graceful exit *or* a channel switch.

### Touch 3 (5 days after touch 2)

**EN:**
> Last note from me here — wanted to share one thing in case it's useful: [one specific market data point or trend relevant to their business, in 1 sentence]. Happy to send the source PDF by email if you'd like. Otherwise I'll leave you alone.

Why it works: gives value with no ask. The "I'll leave you alone" is a permission play — many people reply because of it.

### Touch 4 (14 days after touch 3) — optional

Only send if you have a *new* hook (new product, price drop, regulation change). Otherwise stop.

**EN:**
> Hi {name}, separate from my earlier notes — wanted to flag {new_hook}. If relevant, happy to share more. If not, no follow-up from me again.

## Per-region tone overrides

| Region | Tone notes |
|---|---|
| 🇮🇳 India | Direct, formal Mr/Ms acceptable, talk pricing early. WhatsApp voice notes acceptable from message 2 onwards. |
| 🇧🇷 Brazil | Warmer opening ("Tudo bem?"), informal você. Avoid English unless they reply in English. |
| 🇲🇽 / LatAm | Use tu (informal you) unless contact is C-level (then usted). Mention shipping incoterm — they care. |
| 🇦🇪 / GCC | Formal, "Mr/Mrs", avoid Friday/Saturday sends, peak Monday–Wednesday. |
| 🇪🇺 Europe (where allowed) | Short, direct, never bullet lists. Reply rate higher when message reads English-as-second-language clean rather than over-polished. |
| 🇺🇸 USA | Skip pleasantries, lead with specific value, expect "what's your MOQ" reply within hours or never. |
| 🇿🇦 South Africa | English fine, "good day" opener common, mention BBBEE if relevant. |
| 🇯🇵 / 🇰🇷 | Switch to LINE/Kakao — see country-targeting.md, WhatsApp reply rates are too low to bother. |

## What never to send in message 1

- PDF/image attachments (article was right about this)
- Links of any kind (most WhatsApp clients hide messages with links from spam-flagged senders)
- More than 2 questions
- Price quotes ("our price is $X") — wait until they ask
- Generic openers ("Hope this finds you well", "I hope you are doing great") — pattern-matches to spam
- Anything translated by Google Translate without native review

## Reply-handling cheat sheet

| Their reply | Your move |
|---|---|
| "Who is this?" | Re-introduce in 1 line + answer with the source and the specific question. Don't escalate to brochure. |
| "Send catalog" | Send a 2-product summary inline (text), not the PDF. Ask which they want details on. |
| "What's your price?" | Ask volume + destination port + payment terms first. Then quote. |
| "Not interested" | "No problem, removed from list. If anything changes, my contact is here." Done. Do not reply again. |
| "Send sample" | Confirm spec + quantity + their courier account. Sample-bait is common; don't auto-ship. |
| Voice note in language you don't read | Reply in text, ask if English/your-language is OK. Don't fake fluency. |
| No reply after touch 4 | Move to email cadence (different channel, different cadence). Don't WhatsApp again for 6+ months. |
